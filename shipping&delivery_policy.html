Shipping and Delivery Policy

Cloud ECS Infotech is committed to providing a seamless and efficient delivery experience for all internships. This policy outlines the shipping and delivery procedures for internship materials, such as hardware, software, or physical documentation
Shipping and Delivery Procedures
If physical goods or study materials are involved,
1. Shipping Method:
  • Domestic Shipments: For internships within India, we will typically use reliable courier services like FedEx, DHL, or Blue Dart.  
  • International Shipments: For internships outside of India, we will select the most suitable international shipping method based on the destination and the nature of the materials.  
2. Shipping Costs:
  • Shipping costs will generally be covered by Cloud ECS Infotech, especially for essential internship materials. However, for non-essential items or expedited shipping requests, additional charges may apply.  
3. Shipping Timeframes:
  • Domestic Shipments: We aim to ship domestic orders within 2-5 business days of order confirmation.  
  • International Shipments:International shipping times may vary depending on the destination and customs clearance procedures. Please allow additional time for international deliveries.  
4. Tracking Information:
  • Once your order is shipped, we will provide you with a tracking number so you can monitor the progress of your shipment.
5. Delivery Address:
  • Please ensure that the shipping address provided is accurate and complete to avoid delays.
6. Customs Clearance:
  • For international shipments, you may be responsible for any customs duties or taxes imposed by your local authorities.
7. Damaged or Lost Shipments:
  • If you receive a damaged or lost shipment, please contact our customer support team immediately. We will investigate the issue and take appropriate action.
Returns and Exchanges
  • Returns: Returns of internship materials will generally not be accepted unless the item is defective or damaged upon receipt.
  • Exchanges: If an item is incorrect or defective, we will exchange it for the correct item or a replacement.

Please note: This policy may be subject to change without notice. For the most up-to-date information, please refer to our website or contact our customer support team.
